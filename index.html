About Me

Azure-native.
Data-obsessed.

I'm a Data Engineer who designs, builds, and maintains scalable data pipelines and ETL/ELT solutions across the Microsoft Azure ecosystem — from ingesting raw data out of SQL Server, MySQL, and file sources through to Gold-layer Lakehouse models that power business intelligence.

I've led the modernization of legacy on-premises ETL systems into cloud-native platforms, implemented Medallion (Bronze–Silver–Gold) Lakehouse architectures in Microsoft Fabric, and built pipelines processing over a million records a day.

I care about data quality, governance, and performance — and the part of the job where reliable data quietly powers better decisions. Currently a Data Engineer at Hashstack Solutions in Hyderabad.
